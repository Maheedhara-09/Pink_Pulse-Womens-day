How to Take Care of Yourself During a period:

Following these simple habits will help stay clean and comfortable during periods!

    Change Pads or Tampons Regularly

  • Change every 4-6 hours to stay fresh and prevent infections.

    • Wash Hands Before and After

  • Always wash hands before and after changing a pad or tampon.

    • Take a Bath Daily

  • Helps stay clean and reduces odor.

    • Wear Clean, Comfortable Clothes

  • Prefer cotton underwear to absorb sweat and keep dry.

    • Dispose of Pads Properly

  • Wrap in paper and throw in a dustbin, never flush.

    • Stay Hydrated

  • Drinking water helps reduce bloating and discomfort.

    • Keep an Extra Pad in Your Bag

  • Useful in case of an emergency.

    • Use Mild Soap for Cleaning

  • Wash the vaginal area gently, don't use harsh soaps.

    • Trim Nails

  • Helps maintain hygiene while handling pads or menstrual cups.

    • Talk to a Trusted Adult

  • If confused or facing any issues, ask a parent, teacher, or doctor for help.
