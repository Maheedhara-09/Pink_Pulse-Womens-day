Simple Tips to Deal with Cramps

Menstrual cramps occur when the uterus contracts to shed its lining, causing pain due to high levels of prostaglandins. These contractions can reduce blood flow, leading to cramps, and factors like stress can worsen the pain. Managing these can help alleviate discomfort during periods.

Diet Plan

  • Drink warm water or herbal teas (ginger or chamomile) to relax muscles.
  • Eat iron-rich foods like spinach, dates, and lentils to replenish lost blood.
  • Include magnesium-rich foods like bananas, dark chocolate, and nuts to reduce pain.
  • Avoid salty, sugary, and processed foods as they can make bloating worse.

Exercise To Follow

  • Gentle stretching to relax muscles.
  • Light yoga poses like child's pose, cobra pose, and butterfly pose.
  • Walking helps improve blood circulation and reduce cramps.
  • Breathing exercises to reduce stress and ease discomfort.
